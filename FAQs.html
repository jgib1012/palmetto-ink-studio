Frequently Asked Questions

Does Getting a tattoo hurt?

Pain levels vary depending on the placement and individual tolerance. Most people say it feels like something burning against the skin but for some they say it is a pricking pain that is manageable.

How much does a tattoo session cost?

On average be prepared to spend at least $100 but the price varies on the size, design, and the time required on the tattoo.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, we require a non-refundable deposit to secure your appointment. The deposit will be applied toward the final cost of your tattoo.

Is there an age requirement?

Yes, you must be at least 18 years old with a valid ID to get a tattoo. We do not tattoo minors, even with parental consent.

How do I take care of my new tattoo?

Keep your tattoo clean, apply a thin layer of healing ointment, and avoid direct sunlight, swimming, or excessive sweating for at least two weeks.
